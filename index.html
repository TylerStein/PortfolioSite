Originally created for Global Game Jam 2019, "Alexa, Run!" is a small chaotic sidescrolling game about managing the needs of people around your home. With an amazing team of three other talented people, the game was built within 48 hours under the theme "What home means to you". "Alexa, Run!"" was created in Unity with art created in ToonBoom, all the ideas leading up to the final game was a joint effort between all members of the group. We went through a number of prototypes and scrapped ideas before finding landing on one that fit our skills and time constraints. A few months after the Game Jam I began work redeveloping the game virtually from the ground up, optomizing the existing art assets and creating a cleaner codebase that would be better suited for expanding the game further. This optomized version can be found on my GitHub, on itch.io, and right here on my website!
